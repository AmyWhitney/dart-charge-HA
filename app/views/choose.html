Make a one-off payment

Pay without registering for an account. Make a one-off payment for a crossing you’ve just made and for crossings you’ll make in the next 12 months.

The crossing fee depends on your vehicle and account type.

Check which crossings you've paid for

After you’ve paid, you can check which crossings you’ve paid for.

You must set up an account to see any journeys you haven’t paid for.

Register a Dart Charge account

Register a Dart Charge account to use the Crossing at a discounted rate.

To activate an account you must add a minimum of £10 to your balance when you register.

Migrate your DART-Tag account

If you are an existing DART-Tag account holder, you can migrate your DART-Tag account.

You'll need you Personal verification code to migrate your account online (this will be emailed/posted to you).

Apply for a local residents' discount

If you live in the Dartford or Thurrock council boundaries, you can apply for a local residents' discount.

You can’t get a local resident discount if your vehicle is registered to a company. You can only get a discount for 1 vehicle per account.

Further information

Further information can be found online by searching GOV.UK.
