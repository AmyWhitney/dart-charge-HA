{{We're introducing a new remote payment system called Dart Charge to reduce congestion at the Crossing, and this means you'll no longer pay at the barriers.

The Crossing will still be free to use between 10pm and 6am. There will be a range of ways to pay, and you can get a discounted rate by registering an account.

To make sure you’re ready for the change, you can use this site to register and manage a Dart Charge account, migrate a DART-Tag account and apply for a local residents’ discount.

Please be aware that Dart Charge accounts will not be activated until the remote charging scheme goes live later this year.
