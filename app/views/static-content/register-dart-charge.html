To activate an account you must add a minimum of £10 to your balance when you register.

Standard account

Standard accounts are suitable for individuals or private vehicle owners.

You will need

  • Registration number for vehicles(s) you want to register
  • A credit or debit card to make an initial payment of £10 to activate the account
  • UK bank account details if you wish to automatically top up your account by Direct Debit

Commercial account

You can apply for a Commercial Account in your registered company’s name.

This includes account management support and single invoices for multiple vehicles.

You will need

  • Company name
  • Company registration number
  • Registration number for vehicles(s) you want to register
  • A credit or debit card to make an initial payment of £10 to activate the account
  • UK bank account details if you wish to automatically top up your account by Direct Debit

Register a Dart Charge commercial account.
