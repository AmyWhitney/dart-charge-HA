From late November 2014, you’ll no longer pay at the barriers when you use the Dartford Crossing. The new payment service is called Dart Charge. Set up a Dart Charge account now so you can use it in November.

Use the Dart Charge service to:

  • transfer your DART-Tag account to a Dart Charge account (you’ll still pay using DART-Tag until late November)
  • set up and manage a standard or commercial account
  • apply for a local resident discount

What you need to know

Start now

Before you start

The crossing fee depends on your vehicle and account type.

Transfer your DART-Tag account

You’ll need your:

  • DART-Tag account number
  • the verification code you got by post or email

Set up a standard or commercial account

You’ll need:

  • your personal details, or company details for a commercial account
  • details of each vehicle you’re registering
  • a debit or credit card

You must start a standard account with £10. If you close your account, any remaining balance will be refunded.

Register for a standard account if your company isn’t registered with Companies House.

Apply for a local resident discount

You must show evidence - you can scan or photograph your documents, or send them by post for checking.

You’ll need:

  • your vehicle details
  • a current Council Tax bill that matches the address in your application
  • proof you live in Dartford or Thurrock, eg a gas, electricity or water bill (less than 3 months old), bank or credit card statement (less than 3 months old), income support book, rent document or driving licence
  • proof your vehicle is registered in Dartford or Thurrock, eg a copy of your V5C vehicle registration or a vehicle lease in your name
  • a debit or credit card

You can only get a discount for 1 vehicle.

You can’t get a local resident discount if your vehicle is registered to a company.

Set up a standard or commercial account by phone

You can set up your account by phone.

Dart Charge
Telephone: 0300 3000 120
